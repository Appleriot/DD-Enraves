Engraving Process

With the power of a powerful laser, we can engraving on metal, wood, arylic, wallets, purses, and leather. The max height and width of a engraving is one foot. Shipping can be done in North Carolina, South Carolina, and VA.

Custom Phone Cases

We engrave on any phone case. We will buy the phone case and send it to you. Just send us the phone case needed and the color wanted. Black or dark color aren't recommeced.

Custom Shirts

Placing vinly sheet onto any color blank shirt and burning the desired image on it without damaging the shirt.

Custom Stickers

A special picked color vinyl sheet will be cut out and image will be burned out. The stickers are water proof and hard to remove once placed.

Custom Dog Tags

A sticker will be cut out and placed onto a golded dog tag that can be placed one chains or necklances.
